===========
Problem
===========
Design the "People You May Know" feature like on linkedin and FB, based on user data.

===========
Solution 1
===========
for a user A, consider all friends of A.
Let us say {F_1, F_2, ..., F_n}.
Let friends of F_i that are not friends of A be NF_i.

Maintain a hashmap P containing the count of suggested friends for A with keys from NF_i s.
Recommend top k from NFs based on their count in P.

===========
Solution 2
===========
In combination to Solution 1 
list of users in persons phone contacts, email contacts, etc.
